Packageorg.mitre.eren.game.utils
Classpublic class MssgQueue
InheritanceMssgQueue flash.display.MovieClip

MssgQueue manages the queue of pending UserMessages. As messages are added to the queue, they are given notification icons corresponding to the priority of the message. Messages stack as they come in, and when a message is finished, other messages in the queue will slide down visually.
